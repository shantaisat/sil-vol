How It Works

Simple steps to start learning

No previous experience needed. No jargon. Just practical, supportive learning at your own pace.

1
Create your free account
Sign up in minutes. Tell us a little about where you're starting from and what you'd like to achieve.
2
Choose your learning pathway
Browse programmes across digital confidence, AI literacy, employability, and more. Filter by level, format, and pace.
3
Learn with support
Access self-paced courses, tutor-led sessions, workshops, and — if you need it — one-to-one volunteer mentoring.
4
Track your progress
Your personal dashboard shows completed learning, achievements, and recommended next steps — at a glance.
