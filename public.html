About SILVOL
Our mission is simple: empower people through learning and opportunity.
SILVOL exists to close the digital divide and help people of all ages and backgrounds build the skills and confidence to thrive in a digital world.
Our Story
SILVOL was created in response to a growing need for accessible, practical digital learning in communities across the UK. We bring together education, technology and human support to deliver life-changing impact.
What We Do
We deliver practical digital skills and AI readiness learning through five connected programme arms.
